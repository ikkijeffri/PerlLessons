Membership

Official Members:

Membership fee is $10; it is valid for one academic year. Paid members will receive a membership card as an official proof of their UTJA membership. Official members are eligible to participate in events during the academic year. However, members may be subjected to an additional fee depending on the event.

Non-Members:

Those who do not hold the official membership are still welcome to participate in events. Non-members will be subjected to a fee, which is variable depending on the event.
